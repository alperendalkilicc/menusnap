MenuSnap - AI-Powered Food Analysis

Last updated: December 2024

1. Introduction

Welcome to MenuSnap. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Photos and Images

MenuSnap analyzes photos of food that you voluntarily submit through the app. These images are processed to provide food analysis results including ingredients, nutritional information, and price estimates.

2.2 Analysis Results

We store your food analysis history locally on your device to provide you with a personal history of your analyses. This includes:

  • Food identification results
  • Nutritional information
  • Price estimates
  • Analysis timestamps

2.3 Technical Information

We may collect technical information necessary for app functionality, including:

  • Device type and operating system
  • App version and crash reports
  • Usage analytics (anonymized)

3. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to:

  • Provide AI-powered food analysis services
  • Maintain your personal analysis history
  • Improve app performance and user experience
  • Provide customer support

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Local Storage

Your analysis history is stored locally on your device. We do not upload or store your personal analysis data on external servers.

4.2 Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • OpenAI API: For food analysis processing (images are processed but not stored)
  • RevenueCat: For subscription management and analytics

4.3 Security Measures

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your data, including encryption for data transmission and secure storage practices.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Your food photos and analysis results remain private and are not shared with other users or external parties.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your stored analysis data within the app
  • Delete your analysis history through the app settings
  • Request information about data processing
  • Uninstall the app to remove all local data

7. Children's Privacy

MenuSnap is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy in the app and updating the "Last updated" date.

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:
