‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How accurate is the food recognition?
A: MenuSnap uses advanced AI models to provide highly accurate food identification. Accuracy depends on photo quality and lighting conditions. Clear, well-lit photos yield the best results.
Q: Are price estimates accurate?
A: Price estimates are based on regional averages and restaurant data. Actual prices may vary depending on location, restaurant type, and local market conditions.
Q: How do I get the best analysis results?
A: For optimal results: ensure good lighting, capture the entire dish, avoid shadows, and keep the camera steady. Multiple angles can help if the initial result isn't satisfactory.
Q: Can I use MenuSnap offline?
A: MenuSnap requires an internet connection for AI analysis. However, your analysis history is saved locally and can be viewed offline.
Q: What about my privacy?
A: Your photos are processed securely and not stored on our servers. Analysis history is saved locally on your device. Read our Privacy Policy for complete details.
Q: How do I cancel my subscription?
A: You can cancel your subscription anytime through your device's App Store settings. Go to Settings > [Your Name] > Subscriptions > MenuSnap > Cancel Subscription.
